Customers Care Program 2016

In our Customers Care Program 2016, Nhu Linh Co. Ltd has organized trips to Thailand (Jan 2016), Japan (July 2016) and the U.S (Aug 2016).

Through the program, we hope to show our gratitude towards our customers with outstanding sale records in the year 2015 - 2016.

The program includes visits to famous landmarks and other social activities to reinforce our relationship with our customers.
