About us


Mr Quy Phan, founder and CEO, in front of Nhu Linh Co. Ltd main office

Our business

Nhu Linh Co., Ltd is the leading fertilizer distributor in Lam Dong Province, Vietnam. From a small retailer in the 90s, we have grown steadily over time. For the time being, our distribution network has covered Lam Dong Province (where your Vietnamese coffee is most likely to come from) and nearby provinces. We distribute to over 500 retailers and farms.
We provide a diverse range of products, of over 100, to meet the requirement for each specific type of soil and stages of growth of plants.
Our products include NPK of different formulae, organic fertilizers... that come in liquid, powder, granular and crystalized form.

We specialise in fertilizers import and distribution with full logistics.
Our main office is located at 350, Quarter 4, Lien Nghia, Duc Trong, Lam Dong Province, Vietnam.

Our two warehouses are located at:

  • Bong Lai, Duc Trong, Lam Dong Province, Vietnam (gross area 6000m2)
  • Loc An, Lam Dong Province, Vietnam (gross area 30002)

Our mission

We commit to provide our farmers with high-quality fertilizers with competitive price.

We are also building connections with worldwide fertilizers producers and specialists, researching and developing technology and formulae to improve crop productivity in Vietnam.

We have been partner with leading fertilizers suppliers in Vietnam such as PetroVietnam, Duc Giang Chemical JSC, Binh Dinh Argriculture JSC, to organise talks and conferences and bring useful information on agricultural techniques so that our farmers can make the right choice for their crops.
