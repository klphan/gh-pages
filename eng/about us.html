About us


Welcome to Nhu Linh Co. Ltd, a family owned company, since 1995, specialising in fertiliser distribution in Vietnam.

Foundation

Nhu Linh Co., Ltd is the leading fertilizer distributor in Lam Dong Province, Vietnam. From a small retailer in the 90s, we have grown steadily over time. In October 2000, we officially locate our office at 350, Quarter 4, Lien Nghia, Duc Trong, Lam Dong Province.
We provide a diverse range of products, of over 100, to meet the requirement for each specific type of soil and stages of growth of plants.
Our products include NPK of different formulae, organic fertilizers... that come in liquid, powder, granular and crystalized form.
